Nestled in the Himalayan mountain range, the region is blessed with picturesque landscapes that captivate visitors. Kashmir is famous for its pristine lakes, including the stunning Dal Lake in Srinagar, where visitors can experience the serene beauty of floating gardens and traditional houseboats called shikaras. Iconic landmarks like the Mughal Gardens in Srinagar, with their symmetrical terraced beauty, add to the charm of the region.The region's four distinct seasons further enhance its allure. King of Kashmiri dishes

* G U S H T A B A

Gustaba, the last dish that’s served in Wazwan holds a lot of importance as refusing it is seen as an insult.This traditional dish sees loaded with authentic Kashmiri flavors. Gushtaba Recipe is an integral part of Kashmiri Wazwan.It is a delicious preparation of meatballs in a thick yogurt gravy. You can serve this aromatic gravy along with Tava roti or butter garlic naan. The recipe has authentic flavours of rich Kashmiri cuisine. Traditionally Gushtaba is served as the last dish of the banquet before the dessert. It is also known as The Dish of Kings in Kashmiri region.
